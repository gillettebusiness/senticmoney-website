Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Website Cookies (senticmoney.com)

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., remembering your language preference)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site through anonymous data collection

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies that follow you across other websites.

Software Application (SenticMoney Desktop App)

Important: The SenticMoney desktop application does not use cookies at all. All your data is stored locally in a database file on your computer, not in browser cookies.

Third-Party Cookies

When you use our payment checkout (Stripe), they may set their own cookies according to their privacy policy. We don't control these cookies.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies (some website features may not work)
  • Delete cookies after closing your browser
  • Allow cookies only from sites you visit directly

Visit your browser's help section for specific instructions on managing cookies.

Questions?

If you have questions about our cookie policy, contact us at contact@senticmoney.com
