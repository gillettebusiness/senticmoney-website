Features That Put You in Control

Powerful financial tracking. Zero compromises on privacy.

SenticMoney Genie

Powered by Google Gemini — choose 3.1 Pro (powerful) or 3.1 Flash-Lite (faster) Standard. The Genie has been trained on every feature in the app — every button, every form field, every workflow. It doesn't just know what page you're on — it can walk you through the exact steps to set up budgets, configure Runway periods, manage bills, and import bank statements.

  • "How does my spending compare to last month?" — instant financial analysis
  • "Walk me through setting up Runway" — guides you through every step and field
  • Knows every button and field — "Click Create Budget, pick a category, enable Carry Forward to roll unused amounts"
  • Compares months, detects anomalies, tracks budget pressure
  • Detects what you've set up and guides you through the rest — from first transaction to full Runway planning
  • Ask questions in English, Spanish, or French — type or speak, Genie responds in your language
  • Voice input — tap the microphone to speak your question instead of typing. Supports English, Spanish, and French.
  • Attach files — drag or clip CSV, Excel, PDF, or images into chat. Genie analyzes bank statements, compares data, and extracts receipt details from photos.
  • Clickable navigation — when Genie mentions a page or transaction, it's a live link. Click and you're there.
  • Money Flow Sankey chart — visualize exactly how income flows to each expense category Standard
  • Voice input on Notes fields — dictate notes hands-free on transactions and other forms in EN, ES, or FR.
