# SenticMoney Article Writing — Quick Reference

**Authoritative writing rules:** `_docs/ARTICLE-WRITING-GUIDE.md`
**Product context and site structure:** `CLAUDE.md`

---

## BEFORE WRITING ANYTHING

1. **Check ARTICLE-STATUS.txt** — confirm the slug is not already published or scheduled. Do not write duplicates.
2. **Identify the article type** — comparison, best-of, method guide, or feature showcase. The type determines which positioning rules apply.
3. **State your plan first** — output the proposed slug, H1, answer capsule, and section outline. Wait for approval before writing the full HTML.

---

## NAV ITEMS

All articles must include exactly **10 items** in both the desktop nav and mobile menu:

> Home, Features, Pricing, Blog, Videos, Download, Live Demo, Support, Books, Map
