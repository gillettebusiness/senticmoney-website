How It Works

1. Download

Install SenticMoney on your Windows PC or Mac. No account required.

2. Import

Import bank statements (CSV/Excel) or add transactions manually.

3. Ask Questions

"How does this month compare to last?" The AI sees your complete financial picture and answers instantly.

Why Choose SenticMoney?

AI That Understands You

Powered by Google Gemini — choose 3.1 Pro or 2.5 Flash. The Genie knows every feature inside and out — ask it to walk you through setting up budgets, configuring Runway periods, or importing bank statements, and it guides you through the exact buttons and fields.

Your Data Stays Yours

Unlike Mint or YNAB, your financial data never leaves your device. No cloud storage, no data mining, no targeted ads.

Affordable Plans

Free tier available with core features. The Standard plan starts at just $39/year. No expensive monthly subscriptions.

Smart Import and Categorization

Import bank statements (CSV, XLSX, OFX, QFX, PDF) with automatic categorization. AI learns your spending patterns.

Receipt Scanning

Scan receipts from any device on your network. AI extracts itemized data — merchant, date, line items, totals — so you can track exactly what you bought.

Personal Setup Coach

The AI is trained on every feature — every button, form field, and workflow. Ask "How do I set up Carry Forward?" or "What should I do next?" and it teaches you step by step, like having the developer sitting next to you.

Free Financial Calculators

Plan your debt payoff and savings goals. Loan payoff, credit card payoff, compound interest, and snowball vs avalanche—all free.

Multi-Device Access

Install on one PC, access from any device on your home network — iPhone, iPad, Mac, Android. No cloud sync needed.

Runway Cash Flow

Know exactly how much you have to live on until your next paycheck. Plan payday-to-payday with Living Money and $/Day metrics.
